Sathya's Favorite Animal Peacock

Peacock is one of the most beautiful birds on earth.
Peacock usually has a blue color and its feathers have a mixture of blue, green, and golden colors.
Peacocks are found in India, Sri Lanka, Indonesia, Africa, etc.
Peacocks look attractive due to their colorful feathers.
Peacock is a national bird in India

wikipedia

Peacock are awesome because...

  1. Peacocks are not picky eaters, and they can eat a broad range of foods because they are omnivorous.
  2. When a peacock is raised from an early age by humans, it may see those humans as its parents.
  3. Peacocks will adapt to changeable weather and climates, despite their tropical and exotic appearance.
  4. One obvious reason peacocks make good pets is the fact that they are beautiful.
  5. When peacocks sense danger approaching, they make a screeching sound.
